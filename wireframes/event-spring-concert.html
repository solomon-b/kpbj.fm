About This Event

Get ready for KPBJ's biggest fundraising event of the year! This spring concert brings together the best of Shadow Hills' underground music scene for one incredible night of live performances, community connection, and support for independent radio.

Every dollar raised goes directly toward upgrading our broadcast equipment, expanding our reach, and ensuring KPBJ can continue to provide a platform for underground voices and experimental music that you won't hear anywhere else on the dial.

What to Expect:

  • 4+ hours of non-stop live music from local and regional artists
  • Special performances by KPBJ hosts stepping out from behind the mic
  • Silent auction featuring rare vinyl, concert tickets, and local art
  • Food trucks and local vendors supporting the community
  • All-ages venue with designated spaces for different vibes
  • Professional sound system and lighting for an unforgettable experience
